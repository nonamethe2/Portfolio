Wild Runner - the Quickening Info

Description

Run, jump, and slide to greatness in this run for glory. Be prepared to face all manner of similar looking obstacles; but fear not there is always a way around. Splitscreen gameplay up to two players can play competitively or non-competitively in a more sandbox oriented gamemode. Lastly, the player character can be customized with different colors and more importantly, the player can equip a variety of hats. In addition to hats the player can also now equip a wide variety of accessories on their face. splitscreen gameplay up to two players allows for competitive and non-competitive gameplay depending on the selected gamemode. There are currently three available modes to choose from: sandbox, elimination, and challenge. If interested please follow the link to the store page for more information: Store
